SING STREET (12A)

"A boy growing up in Dublin during the 1980s escapes his strained family life by starting a band to impress the mysterious girl he likes."

Written and directed by Irish-born filmmaker John Carney, and starring Ferdia Walsh-Peelo and Lucy Boynton, Sing Street was nominated for the Best Musical or Comedy at the 2016 Golden Globes.
